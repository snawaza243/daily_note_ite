ЁЯУЭ Lyrics in English (Translation)

A prayer rises to my lips as my heart's desire
May my life shine like a candle, O Lord

May my breath forever bring glory to my homeland
Just like flowers bring beauty to a garden
Let my life be like a moth drawn to the flame
Let me fall in love with the light of knowledge, O Lord

May my work be to support the poor
And to show love to those in pain and the elderly

O Allah, keep me away from wrongdoing
Guide me on the path of righteousness
